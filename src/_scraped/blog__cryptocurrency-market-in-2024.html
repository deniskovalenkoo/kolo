What is expected of the cryptocurrency market in 2024?

For the long term, the cryptocurrency market is expected to have significant growth and alterations. As we consider the past year’s events, it is apparent that the landscape of digital currency evolution is always changing. Let's explore the most recent innovations and important trends in the world of cryptocurrency.

 

2023 Crypto Recap

2023 has been a thrilling ride for the cryptocurrency world. This time period saw a significant increase in Bitcoin's popularity, there were groundbreaking advances in blockchain technology, and an increase in the market's enthusiasm that was greater than anyone originally predicted.

 

Here's a summary of the significant events that impacted the cryptocurrency ecosystem in this year:

- Bitcoin Ordinals: February 2023 marked the start of Bitcoin's Ordinals. This cutting-edge concept facilitated the inclusion of all content on satoshis, the smallest units of Bitcoin. Unlike previous NFTs that were built on top of separate blockchain layers, Ordinals are incorporated into Bitcoin transactions, this represents a significant advancement in the crypto world.

 

- The European Union's MiCA Regulation: In a significant development, April 2023 saw the European Union formalize the regulation of Crypto-Assets (MiCA). This precedented the introduction of global regulations regarding crypto, these regulations sought to provide a standardized framework for regulating investors and maintaining stability in the market.

 

- The Ripple Effect: July 2023 was the date of the SEC's formal failure against Ripple. The decision, which distinguished the way Ripple sold to institutional investors and consumers on the retail side, had a significant impact on the classification of cryptocurrencies as securities.

 

- PayPal's Foray and ETF Developments: August 2023 saw PayPal introduce its cryptocurrency-based stablecoin, this further enhanced the integration of cryptocurrencies into mainstream payment systems. Additionally, a U.S. court decision favoured the application of Grayscale's Bitcoin ETF, this decision represented a significant progression towards greater participation in the crypto market by institutions.

 

- The G20's Unified Regulatory Framework: The adoption of a common regulatory scheme for cryptocurrencies in October 2023 was considered a significant development. It sought to standardize the regulations across member countries, this would reduce the fragmentation of the market and provide a blueprint for the regulations until 2025.

 

- The Price Dynamics of Bitcoin and the progress of regulatory agencies: As 2023 came to an end, the price of Bitcoin continued to increase in value while people were awaiting the approval of an ETF. Regulatory increases in various countries, including the compliance directive in India and the permission of crypto advertisements by Google in the U.S., demonstrated the ongoing evolution of the crypto regulatory landscape.

 

Overall, 2023 demonstrated the cryptocurrency sector's progress and viability as it traversed regulatory issues, technological advances, and market dynamics. The industry's resilience and adaptability suggests a new era of growth and integration into the global financial system.

 

2024 Crypto Prediction

In 2024, a recent forecast expects Bitcoin (BTC) to reach a new all-time high, with estimates of reaching $88,000 and having a total value of $77,000 by the end of the year. The current cost of Bitcoin is approximately $67,658 (as per May 29, 2024). A research study by the UK-based fintech company Finder has predicted that Bitcoin will have an average price of $87,875 by the end of 2024.

 

Kadan Stadelmann, CTO of Komodo, says that Bitcoin's potential for success in 2024 is attributed to multiple factors. Firstly, large corporations and institutional investors are taking an interest in Bitcoin that is likely to increase demand. Second of all, the approval of spot ETFs increases the accessibility of bitcoin prices. This is different from the previous market cycles.

 

Experts believe that Bitcoin will have a potential value of $122,688 in 2025 and $366,935 in 2030. However, the average price that is expected by the truncated mean is around $220,708 by 2030.

 

Conclusion

As we travel through the evolving landscape of the cryptocurrency industry in 2024, it's important to remain aware of emerging trends and developments.

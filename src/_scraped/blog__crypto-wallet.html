What is a crypto wallet?

What is a crypto wallet?

A crypto wallet is a digital account that is used to store, transfer and receive cryptocurrencies. It's similar to a traditional wallet, but instead of physical cash or cards, it works with digital currency.

 

A cryptocurrency wallet allows users to manage their digital assets. In the article "What is cryptocurrency?" we previously established that cryptocurrencies are decentralized, which means that they lack a central authority to regulate them. As a result, users require a safe space to store their cryptocurrency assets and conduct transactions. A crypto wallet offers security and control, it allows users to send and receive cryptocurrencies without the assistance of a third party.

 

According to the methods of keeping cryptocurrencies, there are two types of wallets: cold and hot. A cold crypto wallet is a physical device that functions without the internet and is completely offline. Hot crypto wallets are devices connected to the internet, thus they can be accessed easily and quickly. Cold wallets include hardware and paper wallets, while hot wallets are associated with software wallets. Below, we'll discuss each of these wallets.

 

Types of crypto wallets

Hardware wallets

Hardware wallets are physical devices that store your personal keys in an offline manner. These wallets are available in a variety of shapes and sizes, but they all have the same function. They create private keys and store them on your device, which facilitates the access of your funds when you need it.

Examples of hardware wallets: Ledger Nano S, Trezor, KeepKey.

 

Advantages of hardware wallets:

➕ Not vulnerable to hackers since it is not connected to the internet.

➕ Store private keys offline.

➕ They can be connected to a computer or mobile device via USB or Bluetooth for use.

➕ Supports multiple cryptocurrencies and is compatible with multiple operating systems.

➕ You can restore access using a special phrase (seed phrase).

 

Disadvantages of hardware wallets:

➖ Hardware wallets cost more compared to other types of wallets.

➖ A physical device is required to access your funds.

➖ If the seed phrase is lost, access cannot be restored.

➖ Vulnerable to physical harm.

 

Software wallets

Software wallets are virtual wallets that can be utilized via a computer or mobile device. These wallets are more practical than hardware wallets because they can be accessed from anywhere with access to the Internet.

Examples of software wallets: Exodus, Coinbase Wallet, Guarda.

 

Advantages of software wallets:

➕ Always available as they can be downloaded to your computer or mobile device.

➕ Usually free or cheap compared to hardware wallets.

➕ Supports multiple cryptocurrencies and is compatible with multiple operating systems.

➕ Easy to use and offers multiple features: transaction history, sharing, etc.

​

Disadvantages of software wallets:

➖ Vulnerable to hackers because private keys are stored on devices connected to the Internet.

➖ Reliance on third-party providers, which can pose risks if the provider is hacked or malfunctions.

➖ Malware can infect your computer or mobile device and steal your private keys.

➖ They do not offer backup and recovery options, which may pose risks if the device is lost or damaged.

 

Paper wallets

Paper wallets are a form of cryptocurrency wallet that involves printing your private key on paper.

Examples of paper wallets: BitAddress, WalletGenerator.net.

 

Advantages of paper wallets:

➕ Like hardware wallets, paper wallets also store private keys offline, making them difficult for hackers to access.

➕ They are a cost-effective way to store cryptocurrency because they are free to create and use.

➕ Does not rely on third-party providers, giving users complete control over their funds.

➕ You can easily create a backup by writing your private key on a piece of paper and keeping it in a safe place.

​

Disadvantages of paper wallets:

➖ Users need to manually enter the private key on the exchange in order to make a transaction.

➖ Unable to access paper wallet = Losing the access to your funds.

➖ Creating and using a paper wallet requires some technical knowledge.

➖ Not as convenient as software and hardware wallets for daily use.

 

Custodial and non-custodial wallets

In the article "Crypto exchange VS crypto wallet: which is better?" we've discussed the specifics and differences between these wallets as well as their benefits and drawbacks. Again, you have control over your private keys in a non-custodial wallet, but a third party has control over it in a custodial wallet. Both options have pros and cons, which can be found in the article mentioned earlier.

 

Choosing the best wallet

When choosing the appropriate wallet, there are several aspects to consider.

 

1. Security. This should be your number one priority. It's crucial to choose a wallet that provides the greatest degree of protection for your digital assets. It's important to use a wallet that has safe features like security, and takes extra steps like backing up your wallet and utilizing two-factor authentication to increase safety.

 

2. Ease of use. You need a wallet that is simple to utilize and always accessible. If the procedure of having a wallet is too difficult or time-consuming, users may use the wallet less often.

 

3. Compatibility with various cryptocurrencies. It's vital to choose a wallet that supports specific cryptocurrencies, as well as keeping an eye on the latest developments in the field to ensure that your wallet is still compatible with new cryptocurrencies. The compatibility of your wallet with multiple cryptocurrencies can guarantee accessibility, flexibility and a perspective on your cryptocurrency assets.

 

Conclusion

A crypto wallet is crucial for those who want to invest in or use cryptocurrencies. Whether you're an experienced user or just beginning your crypto journey, a crypto wallet can assist you in keeping your finances safe and providing you with the freedom to utilize your cryptocurrency as you'd like. Ensure that you choose a dependable and safe cryptocurrency wallet that satisfies your requirements. With the appropriate wallet, you can take advantage of all the benefits of digital currency without worrying about the associated risks of traditional financial systems.
